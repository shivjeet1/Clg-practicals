About the Institute

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering (SSGMCE), Shegaon, was established in 1983 by Shri Gajanan Shikshan Sanstha. It is affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, approved by AICTE and recognized by DTE Maharashtra.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a center of excellence in technical education and research.

Mission: To produce engineers and professionals with sound technical knowledge, ethical values, and leadership qualities.

Principal's Message

We at SSGMCE believe in nurturing talent and creating technically sound professionals ready for global challenges.
