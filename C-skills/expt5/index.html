About Akola

Akola is a vibrant city located in the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra. Known as the "Cotton City", Akola plays a key role in the state's agricultural sector. It's a rapidly developing city with a blend of culture, heritage, and modernity.

Famous Places

  • Raj Rajeshwar Temple
  • Narnala Fort
  • Shegaon (nearby, home of Shri Gajanan Maharaj)
  • Balapur Fort
  • Shahanur Dam

Culture & Festivals

Akola celebrates all major festivals like Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Ganesh Chaturthi with enthusiasm. It is known for its delicious street food and local produce. The city is culturally rich with traditional folk music and dance seen during festive times.
